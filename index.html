03
Execute 2 Large Projects This Year
Priority targets: Thwake Dam Kenya (₹2–3.5 Cr) and LHWP Phase 2 South Africa (₹2.5–4 Cr). Both are under active construction. Bid as named instrumentation sub-consultant — this is the only way to be inside the contract and not just a vendor on a shortlist.
04
Expand into New Territories
Kenya, Morocco, Nigeria and Ghana are net-new markets with zero existing ERMS revenue. Identify high-value dealers and service providers, build direct client relationships, and have an active revenue-generating project pipeline in each new market before end of FY 2026–27.
05
Quarterly Pipeline Discipline
Live, dated pipeline for every market — reviewed every quarter without exception. Every entry must have: project stage, estimated value, specific product need, a named contact, and a next action with a committed date. No entry without an owner and a deadline.
06
Hire 2 Dedicated Sales People — Q1
Two regional sales hires to be in place before end of Q1:

1 · Southern Africa — covering South Africa, Botswana, Namibia. Based in Johannesburg.

2 · Northern & West Africa — covering Egypt, Morocco, Nigeria. Based in Cairo or Casablanca.
